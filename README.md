## Overview

FinTwin is an AI-powered financial simulation platform that helps users understand the future impact of their financial decisions.

Unlike traditional expense-tracking applications that focus on historical data, FinTwin creates a digital financial twin and simulates potential outcomes based on user behavior, spending habits, savings patterns, and financial goals.

The objective is simple:

**Help users make smarter financial decisions before they make them.**

---

## Problem Statement

Most students and young professionals make financial decisions without understanding their long-term consequences.

Current financial applications primarily:

* Track past expenses
* Visualize historical spending
* Lack future forecasting
* Do not simulate financial decisions

As a result, users often struggle to evaluate how today's choices affect tomorrow's financial health.

FinTwin was developed to solve this gap using Artificial Intelligence and predictive analytics.

## Features

### 🔮 Future Financial Prediction

Forecast future savings, expenses, and financial trends using AI-driven models.

### 🧪 Scenario Simulation

Simulate different financial decisions and evaluate their outcomes before taking action.

Generate a personalized financial wellness score based on spending behavior, savings consistency, and financial stability.

### 🎯 Goal Planning

Create and track financial goals with intelligent recommendations for achieving them.

## System Workflow

1. User enters financial information.
2. FinTwin creates a digital financial profile.
3. AI models analyze financial behavior.
4. Future scenarios are generated.
5. Financial health metrics are calculated.
6. Personalized recommendations are delivered.

---

## Key Innovation

Traditional finance applications answer:

❓ "Where did my money go?"

FinTwin answers:

✅ "What will happen if I make this financial decision?"

By creating a digital financial twin, users can experiment with decisions before taking real-world financial risks.
